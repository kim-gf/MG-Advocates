Planning, Environment, Local Government & Land Court

Michael Gitonga & Company Advocates provides specialized legal services in Planning, Environment, Local Government & Land Court matters. Our attorneys are well-versed in the complexities of environmental regulations and land use laws.

Our team is committed to providing thorough legal guidance to ensure compliance with planning and environmental laws, protecting the interests of our clients and the community.
