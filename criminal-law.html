Criminal Law - General

At Michael Gitonga & Company Advocates, our Criminal Law practice is dedicated to defending individuals accused of crimes. Our experienced legal team ensures that your rights are protected at every stage of the criminal justice process.

We handle a wide range of criminal cases including:

  • Theft and robbery
  • Assault and battery
  • Drug offenses
  • White-collar crimes
  • Domestic violence
  • Homicide

Our approach is thorough and strategic, focusing on building a strong defense to achieve the best possible outcome for our clients.
