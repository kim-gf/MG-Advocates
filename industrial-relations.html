Industrial Relations, Unions & Employment Law

At Michael Gitonga & Company Advocates, we provide expert legal services in Industrial Relations, Unions & Employment Law. Our attorneys are experienced in handling disputes and negotiations between employers and employees.

We offer legal support in areas such as:

  • Employment contracts
  • Labor disputes
  • Union negotiations
  • Workplace policies
  • Disciplinary actions
  • Employee benefits

Our goal is to foster positive industrial relations and ensure compliance with employment laws, protecting the rights of both employers and employees.
