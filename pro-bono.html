Pro Bono Legal Services

At Michael Gitonga & Company Advocates, we believe in giving back to the community through our Pro Bono Legal Services. We offer free legal assistance to those who cannot afford to pay for representation, ensuring that everyone has access to justice.

Our Pro Bono services include:

  • Legal representation in court
  • Legal advice and consultation
  • Assistance with legal documentation
  • Community legal education

We are committed to making a positive impact on the lives of those in need, providing dedicated and compassionate legal support.
